here is sturcture of [edit pattern]

           |   A      |   B      |    C     |

 0 .... |..| ... .... | ... .... | ... .... |
 1 .... |..| ... .... | ... .... | ... .... |
 2 .... |..| ... .... | ... .... | ... .... |

 ^   ^    ^   ^  ^^^^    ^  ^^^^    ^  ^^^^
 1   2    3   4  5678    4  5678    4  5678

1. number of edit line.
2. data of envelope (it's zx-spekka bass! really kewl! :)
3. data of noise for notes in all channels (A,B,C).
4. place for note (forexaple: C-7 - it's mean... u're know,
   aren't it? :) '7' - it's number of octave (8 octaves)).
5. number of sample (31| 1,2,3...a,b,c... t,u,v).
6. mask for note ('F') or for envelope (envelope=bass: 'E','A' -
   sounds mellow; 'C','8' - sounds harsh and rough).
7. place for ornament (1-F not decimal). :)
8. place  for  volume  (max - 'F', other words 15 (F) levels, 0
   (empty place) - without changes).

[edit sample]

 0  ...  -.... |..| T-- ############### F
 1  ...  -.... |..| T-- ############### F
 2  ...  -.... |..| T-- ##############. E
 3  ...  -.... |..| T-- #############.. D
[4  ...  -.... |..| T-- #############.. D
 5] -..  -.... |..| T-- #############.. D

that example of easy sample, just sounds Tone (T)

ok, here is structure of sample:

[0] ...  -.... |..| TNE ############### F

 ^  ^^^    ^    ^   ^^^                 ^
 1  234    5    6   789                 A

1. number of sample line (0-63) with loops (as ornament).
2. '+'  -  volume's encreasing up and '-' - volume's decreasing
   down.
3. that's activate slide (up/down) for '5' (hard to translate).
4. that's activate slide (up/down) for '6'.
5. it  can  be  used in hard samples with '3', forexample, drum
   (it'll  down  text).  u  may  set  here a frequence for note,
   that's  main trouble on 'way of muzaker' :) try to understand
   it in your practise.
6. data of Noise (N) (0-31 - decimal)
7. 'T' - Tone, sounds clear. (great sound:)
8. 'N' - Noise, sounds as noise :)
9. 'E'  -  Envelope  (we  says - 'ogibayushaya' ;) if 'on' than
   sample will sounds as bass (mask ('6' - edit pattern) must be
   not 'F').
A. volume of sample [0-F (15) not decimal]
