A change of plan you might say!!!!! Well here at Subliminal     
Extacy we have decided to make this 'fanzine' a 128k uni-disk   
mag and not just a +D mag as we first set out to do!!!!  So if  
in the first few articles it goes on about +D only then you know
that it isn't +D only but 128k only.                            
                                                                
This change has come about because of a plee from the people of 
Sinclair Classic as they don't own a +D but would love to have a
copy of the mag!!!!  So after they asked for a 128k tape version
i got out good old tom packer and started to see how well every 
thing compresses!!!!  And hopefully now I can squeeze upto and  
over 50k of text into 1 memory page (16k) and I have 5 free     
memory pages to play with so this version can have over 250k of 
text in it!  You may say that speccy has 8 memory pages! Yes but
page 18 is the ROM and page 21 is the screen+basic.  But that   
still leaves 6 and not 5, well the other page holds the menu    
code and the music etc...                                       
                                                                
I think that is all I have to say so enjoy this mag and write an
article for the next issue!!!  Read other articles on where to  
send them to etc.....
