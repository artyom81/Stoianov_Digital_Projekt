Subliminal Extacy 01 - The New Improved Grafix Mode
>>>>> The New Improved Grafix Mode <<<<<            
                                                                
                    -= By L.A. Of Extacy-3 =-                   
                                                                
                                                                
Yes!  That's right!  Subliminal Extacy now has a new improved   
grafix mode.  The new improved grafix mode sits in the character
spaces 128 to 143 (U.D.G.'s) and is very special, as I have not 
yet seen it in any Spectrum Disk or Tape-zines.                 
                                                                
It all started one fine morning in my house when I decided that 
med-res U.D.G.'s were too limited and looked very blocky.  So   
being the artist I am, I set to work on a new grafix mode.      
After several crap idea had been rejected by myself, I was left 
with a final idea.  Which was to have grafix drawn with lines.  
I then designed the new grafix set and started to try them out. 
I soon found out that having just horizontal and vertical lines 
was very crap and restricting.  So I designed two diagonal      
lines.  But this left gaps in places on some logos.  So four    
more diagonal lines with horizontal lines on them had to be     
designed.  And then finally the new grafix set was finished!!!
