1-3 - Wrecked Tangles
I have a fascination with randomly-generated patterns especially if the program is quite simple. WRECKED TANGLES has been slightly modified from a very early routine I did (back in 1983). It was one of the things I did when first exploring BASIC. Examine the listing to see how it's done. These pattern generators leave some people cold, others get absorbed in watching! If you enjoy them and have done anything similar then send it in to OUTLET! - Brian Cavers
