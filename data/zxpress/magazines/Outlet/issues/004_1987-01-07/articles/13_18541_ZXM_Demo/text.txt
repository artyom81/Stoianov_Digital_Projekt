OUTLET is a compilation of news, programs, articles, information, routines, utilities, letters and much more, all on a monthly disc or microdrive cartridge. 
OUTLET is a DRIVE magazine, that is, it is supplied on and  it is designed to work with, both disc drives and microdrives. The many items in the magazine  can  thus be accessed  quickly and easily, either from the CONTENTS page or from item to item. 
OUTLET  originated in September, 1987 on microdrive cartridge and is now also available  on a 3.5" disc for use with OPUS DISCOVERY but we also hope to make  OUTLET available on other drive systems such as  DISCIPLE  and SWIFTDISC and possibly the SPECTRUM+3. 
If you would like to be informed when OUTLET is available on your disc system,  please leave  your name and address and the name of your system with us. 
The microdrive version of OUTLET will expand in  January, 1988 to well over  200K  (compressed  to about 180K), on twin cartridges, to bring it into  line  with the capacity of the disc version.
