[final, complete & the only ones]
PHAT0  party,  dedicated  to  scene and music, took place on
22nd  of september in the city of Liepaja, Latvia, 2000 AD. This
file  contains  results of music, graphics, 1024 bytes intro and
demo   compos,   which   were  gathered  and  counted  utilising
votesheets  filled  by  humble visitors of the party. Tables are
easy  enough  to  understand,  besides  place, title, author and
points gained, last column is supposed to show what platform the
ware  was  made  for.  Guess what, "spx" stands for speccy while
"c64" is... c64.
