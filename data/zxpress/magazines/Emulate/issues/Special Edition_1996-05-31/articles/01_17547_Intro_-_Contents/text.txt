                   The Magazine For Spectrum emulators
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                           -SPECIAL EDITION 1-
                               WINTER 1995
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Welcome to the first special edition of the regular Spectrum Emulator
Magazine, Emulate!
This special is a real treat for everyone who is having problems with
Speccy games. It is a massive A-Z list of hints, tips, pokes and cheats,
which lists hundreds of games.

These tips were coverted using OCR from the original YS version by DAMION
YATES and were transferred into HTML documents by BEN BAYLIS.
(B.Baylis@cs.ucl.ac.uk) It is from these HTML pages that this magazine is
produced. Thanks very much to both of you.

For those of you who have access to the WWW, you can find the original
HTML version of the following tips at this site. It is definitely worth
checking out!! :-

HTTP://www.cs.ucl.ac.uk/students/zcacbb1/spectrum/smashtips/

These tips were originally presented in:

YOUR SINCLAIR 32 (August 1988)    - Part I (A-F) & Part II (G-N)
YOUR SINCLAIR 33 (Spetember 1988) - Part III (N-S)
YOUR SINCLAIR 34 (October 1988)   - Part IV (S-T)
YOUR SINCLAIR 35 (November 1988)  - Part V (T-Z)
YOUR SINCLAIR 36 (December 1988)  - Part VI (Appendices)
