*  PART 4  - ADVENTURE GAMES  *
*******************************

  This  is  the monthly section devoted to
the  classic  "typing-in"  game.  In  this
part,  we  will  try to answer any queries
you  have  on  an  adventure game and from
time  to  time  run  complete solutions on
difficult games.
  As  this  is the first issue, we have no
queries  to  answer :( but if you have any
problems  for  future issues or would like
to  send  us a complete solution for us to
include,  just  post to the main addresses
titling your letter "ADVENTURE GAMES"

  Just  a small section for Issue.1, heres
a  few  tips for anyone having problems on
the  adventure  game  included  with  this
issue, "Twice Shy" To prevent spoiling the
game,  I  have  encoded  the  answers in a
similar  way  to  Crash. To unencode, just
take A=B, B=C etc....Z=A
